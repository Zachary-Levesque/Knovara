# Customer Personas

## Overview

Knovara is designed to reduce the time required for engineers to become productive inside an organization.

Several stakeholders experience this problem differently.

Understanding these users is critical for building the right product.

---

# Persona 1: Engineering Intern

## Profile

* First internship or early career experience
* Limited organizational context
* Limited industry experience

## Problems

* Doesn't know where to start
* Doesn't know which documentation matters
* Doesn't know who to ask for help
* Doesn't understand company terminology
* Doesn't understand how projects fit together

## Goals

* Become productive quickly
* Gain confidence
* Learn relevant technologies
* Deliver meaningful work

## Current Solutions

* Asking teammates
* Reading documentation
* Trial and error

---

# Persona 2: New Graduate Engineer

## Profile

* Recently graduated
* Strong technical ability
* Limited industry experience

## Problems

* Large amount of unfamiliar information
* Difficulty prioritizing learning
* Unclear ownership and responsibilities
* Slow understanding of organizational structure

## Goals

* Ramp up quickly
* Deliver value
* Build credibility within the team

---

# Persona 3: Experienced Engineer Joining a New Company

## Profile

* Experienced engineer
* Strong technical background
* New to the organization

## Problems

* Understands technology quickly
* Struggles with organizational context
* Needs to understand people, projects, and processes

## Goals

* Become independent quickly
* Understand decision-making processes
* Avoid wasting time searching for information

---

# Persona 4: Senior Engineer

## Profile

* Subject matter expert
* Frequently asked onboarding questions

## Problems

* Constant interruptions
* Repeating the same explanations
* Knowledge trapped in their head

## Goals

* Reduce repetitive onboarding work
* Scale knowledge sharing
* Focus on engineering work

---

# Persona 5: Engineering Manager

## Profile

* Responsible for team productivity

## Problems

* New hires ramp up slowly
* Senior engineers spend time onboarding
* Productivity loss during onboarding

## Goals

* Reduce time-to-productivity
* Improve onboarding experience
* Scale team growth efficiently

---

# Initial Beachhead Customer

The first target customer for Knovara is:

## Engineering Managers

Why?

Engineering managers directly feel the cost of slow onboarding.

They are responsible for:

* Team productivity
* Hiring
* Ramp-up success
* Knowledge transfer

If Knovara can reduce onboarding time, engineering managers are the most likely champions of the product.

---

# Initial End User

The first end users are:

* Interns
* New graduates
* Newly hired engineers

These users experience the onboarding pain most directly and can provide the fastest feedback on product value.
